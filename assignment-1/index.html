Somewhere Over the Rainbow

Performed by Judy Garland

About the Song

Somewhere Over the Rainbow is one of the most iconic songs in cinema history. Written by Harold Arlen with lyrics by E.Y.Harburg, it was composed for the 1939 film The Wizard of Oz and peformed by Judy Garland. The song won the academy Award for best Original Song and has been covered by countless artists over the decades.

About the Artist

Judy Garland, born Frances Ethel Gumm in 1922, was one of the greatest entertainers of the twentieth century. She began her career in vaudeville as a child and was signed to Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer as a teenager. Her potrayal of Dorothy Gale in The Wizard of Oz at age sixteen made her an international star and cemented her place in Hollywood history.
